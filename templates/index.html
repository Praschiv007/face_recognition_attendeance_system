How It Works

  1. Registration: New employees register by providing their details and capturing their face
  2. Check-in/out: Employees simply look at the camera to mark their attendance
  3. Tracking: The system automatically records check-in and check-out times
  4. Reports: View comprehensive attendance reports and statistics
